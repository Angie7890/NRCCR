Phill's Models

Before getting into the cars and the best setups we wanted to mention Phill's Models in North Shields our local model shop - it's great to support guys like these in a time when online purchasing threatens these businesses and the assistance they can provide to racers and modellers alike.

Phill's Models offer us a great service where if you let Phill (or Chris Clarke) know in advance they'll usually get the spares or tyres etc. you need down at the next race meet - this is great value for you and saves on postage - their website (including directions) can be accessed here

Tyres

Best TC Tyres
Schumacher SST Yellow Mini Pin Tyres, SST Yellow Rally Tyres, or Carpet Dragon SST Tyres

Best Buggy Tyres
Schumacher Mini Pin 2 Yellow compound tyres work best for buggies on the wooden gym floor and grip especially well when they start to just fray at the end of the pin (mini pin 1s are not far behind). Alternatively yellow darts and even mini spikes can work well and some like the 'edgier' staggered ribs at the front. The Schumacher Cactus tyres are not suited to this surface nor are tyres with the outer edge pins removed - these being better for the grippier surfaces (more grip roll) like astro or carpet
