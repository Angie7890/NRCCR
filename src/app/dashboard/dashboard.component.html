Newcastle RC Car Racers


Newcastle RC Car Racers is a club for racing 1/10th electric radio controlled cars based near Newcastle in Tyne and Wear.

We race both 1/10th electric touring cars and buggies (2wd and 4wd). Touring Cars are stock class limited to 10.5T (buggies are open). Racing is indoors on a wooden floor sometimes with some carpet sections to mix it up and jumps for the buggies.

Racing is timed using AMBRC. Electric power is provided in the hall (note: lipos must be charged in a lipo bag).

We race every Monday night from 7pm until 10pm and have 4 rounds of qualifying heats followed by 2 finals. Race fees are £6 for members and £7 for non members (juniors £4 / £5). Non members can race as guests for two nights after which BRCA insurance/membership is required.

New racers are very welcome - you'll find everyone here is very friendly and will provide plenty of help and advice - come and try it - you'll love it...!

Please use the links on the left to find out more including where to find us, plus we have a Facebook page where you can ask questions of members and keep up with the latest chat.
